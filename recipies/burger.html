ingridents

  • ground beef
  • burger buns
  • cheese of choice

steps

  1. chop all veggies to liking tomatoes, letttus,onions
  2. heat grill or pan on medium for best results
  3. place burger on grill or pan and flip every two and a half minutes
  4. make a small cut inside to check if burger is cooked tou your liking
  5. once cooked turn down the temperature to low and place cheese ontop
  6. once melted place patty on buns and add toppings and condiments
